## 🔍 Fully Open Source

MultiExodus is **100% open source**.
Every line of code is publicly available in this repository and can be reviewed, audited, or modified by anyone.

* ❌ **Zero** obfuscation
* ❌ **Zero** telemetry
* ❌ **Zero** hidden network requests
* ❌ **Zero** data collection

---

## 🔐 No Access to Your Seed Phrase

MultiExodus **never reads, stores, transmits, or processes your seed phrase**.

When you add or restore a wallet:

* 🤝 The **seed phrase is entered directly into Exodus**
* 🔒 The **Exodus recovery UI** handles everything
* 🔔 MultiExodus only triggers Exodus **built-in recovery mode**
* 💡 All cryptographic operations are performed by **Exodus itself**

👉 **MultiExodus does not intercept keystrokes, memory, clipboard data, or wallet contents.**

---

## 🗂️ What MultiExodus Actually Manages

MultiExodus only works with **wallet files on your local machine**:

* ©️ Copying wallet folders
* 📝 Renaming folders
* 📩 Backing up existing wallets
* 📊 Organizing wallets into separate directories
* 🔨 Loading a selected wallet into Exodus by replacing the active wallet folder

---

## 🛡️ Optional Local Encryption

If enabled, wallet metadata (names, notes, tags, images) can be encrypted locally using **AES-256**.

* 🔐 Encryption happens **on your machine**
* 👁️ The key **never** leaves your system
* ❌ **Nothing** is uploaded or shared

---

## 🌐 No Remote Control

* ❌ **Zero** connection to Exodus servers
* ❌ **Zero** communication with blockchains
* ❌ **Zero** data sent to third parties
* ❌ **Zero** included trackers or analytics

The only optional external connections are:

* 🐈 **GitHub** ➔ Checking for Updates (can be disabled)
* 🌐 **Discord RPC** ➔ Showing activity Status (can be disabled)

---

## 🧠 You Stay in Control

* 🔨 **Control** when wallets are loaded or deleted
* ❗ See **confirmation prompts** before destructive actions
* 👑 Keep **full ownership** of your Exodus installation and files
* 🗑️ Uninstall MultiExodus at any time **without affecting Exodus itself or any Wallets**
